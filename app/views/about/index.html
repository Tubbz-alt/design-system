Contributing to the patterns

For Home Office designers, we have a process for contributing to these patterns. We would be happy for feedback if the contribution process doesn’t work for you.

Alpha pattern

We’ve seen this problem in an area and it is being peer reviewed by the team and iterated upon. The pattern may change quite a lot in alpha. Teams are ok to use this in early prototypes but should not use it in production.

Beta pattern

We’re testing a proposed pattern with two or more delivery teams. The pattern is more mature and robust at this stage. Other teams are free to use this pattern in their own prototypes by adding a comment to say that they will be using it. They must also report back on their research findings.

Live pattern

It’s in production and research has been evidenced to show it works. It still may be iterated and improved upon. Every team must use this version of the pattern.
