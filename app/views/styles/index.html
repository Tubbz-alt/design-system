Styles

We use different design standards, or styles, applied to Home Office services and products depending on what domain they sit in and what kind of service it is.

Home Office design style

Use the Home Office colours, Inter-UI font and styles if your service is:

  • an internal service such as a caseworking system or admin product and
  • sits on a homeoffice.gov.uk domain

Internal service Animal Licensing on homeoffice.gov.uk
Admin product Admin product

Note: for public-facing services on homeoffice.gov.uk domain, independent branding may be used.

GOV.UK design standard

Use the crown logo, GDS Transport font and GOV.UK colours if your service is:

  • public-facing and transactional and
  • sits on a gov.uk or service.gov.uk domain

Public transactional service www.passport.service.gov.uk
Public transactional service www.right-to-work.service.gov.uk

Note: Public-facing services sitting on gov.uk or service.gov.uk must use the GOV.UK design standard unless there is an exemption.

Independent design standard

Independent branding is used on Home Office services that are:

  • marketing or informational sites
  • public-facing services on gov.uk that are exempt from using GOV.UK style
  • public-facing services on the homeoffice.gov.uk domain

Marketing/informational www.joiningthepolice.co.uk
GOV.UK exemptions www.gla.gov.uk
