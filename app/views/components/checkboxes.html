Checkboxes

Smaller checkboxes for multi-use and internal services when the GOV.UK style is too big.

  • Usage: internal
  • Status: experimental
  • Last updated: 19 July 2017

Why have we created this pattern?

GOV.UK styling for check boxes might not always be appropriate for multi-use interfaces.

User research

  • We have identified this might be a need but need further research to confirm.
  • From the small amount of research we have, we haven't identified any issues with the size.
