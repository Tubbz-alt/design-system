Confirmation: Account creation
EXPERIMENTAL

This pattern is currently experimental because more research is needed to validate it.

Use this pattern if the user needs to sign back in to change details on their application, and also be able to save and continue with their application.

When to use this pattern

If the user creates an account whilst making an application, and needs to sign back in to update and change their details. It should also allow the user to continue and save their application at any time.

Services using this pattern

  • Apply for the EU Settlement Scheme
  • Visas and immigration

Research

Further research is needed on how to let users know they can change their account details in the future. And also to look into whether adapting confimation pages is the right format for users to review information more easily.

Accessibility

If your service uses this pattern, we’d be interested to hear any insights you have on any accessibility considerations.
