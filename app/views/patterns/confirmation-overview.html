Confirmation

Use this pattern to let users know they have completed a transaction.

Overview

Confirmation pages reassure users that they have completed a transaction and helps them understand what to expect or what to do next. You should make it clear to the user what stage they are in their application process.  

Use the GOV.UK pattern and guidance for most styles of confirmation pages.

However there are other examples of confirmation pages that are not included on GOV.UK.
